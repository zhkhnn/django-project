You can get answers to frequently asked questions here

  • What is yoga?

    Yoga is the connection of the body, mind and breath through a series of guided poses and sequences. Yoga encourages increased body awareness while also improving strength, flexibility and balance. There are many types of yoga, each with different focuses and benefits, however most revolve around the central idea of connecting the body, mind and breath.

  • What equipment do you need for yoga?

    The equipment you will need for yoga includes a yoga mat, appropriate yoga clothing, and a water bottle. There is some extra yoga equipment that you could also consider including a yoga mat bag, a yoga towel, a yoga strap, a yoga block or brick, and a yoga blanket.

  • What should you wear to yoga?

    You should wear any nonrestrictive clothing to yoga. This can include specific yoga tops, yoga leggings, a sports bra, shorts, etc. Really, wear anything that allows you to move freely and feel comfortable.
